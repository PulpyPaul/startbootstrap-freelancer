Raise the Bass

  • Tech: Unity5, C#, Plastic (Source Control), GameAnalytics, GoogleAdMob

  • Team Size: 6

  • Role: Lead Programmer and Game Designer - Developed class architecture, gameplay, user controls, game state systems, procedurally generated enemies, and level progression. Also integrated ads, analytics tracking, and made adjusts to mechanics based on play tests

  • Description: Raise The Base is a mobile arcade Game for iOS and Android. This application was developed with a team of 6 from scratch. We started with a business model and core mechanic followed by iterative prototyping, playtesing, and eventually launching the beta on the Apple and Google Play store.
